Your Guidance Journey

A simple, structured process to help you get the most out of your counselling experience at the expo.

STEP 1

Register for QELE 2026

Your free student registration is your all-access pass. Once registered, you'll be eligible to book a counselling slot.

STEP 2

Book Your Session

Closer to the event date, you'll receive an email to book a specific time slot with one of our counsellors at the expo.

STEP 3

Prepare for Your Session

Think about your questions, interests, and bring any relevant documents like school transcripts or test scores to make the session highly productive.

STEP 4

Meet Your Counsellor

Attend your scheduled session at the dedicated Career Counselling Zone at the expo and walk away with a clearer action plan for your future.
